TCS Virtual internship# Internship
Overview:
This virtual internship involved working on a data analytics project for an online retail store, using Microsoft Excel, Power BI, and PowerPoint to perform data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and generate actionable business insights. The objective was to provide the CEO and CMO with a data-driven overview of the company’s performance and recommendations for strategic business expansion.

Project Context:
The retail store has been performing well, and the management team wanted to identify the key factors driving revenue growth. They sought insights from both operational and marketing perspectives to understand what’s working, where to improve, and which markets offer expansion opportunities.

Key Tasks & Deliverables:

Task 1 – Business Questions Development:
Prepared a set of focused analytical questions tailored to the information needs of the CEO and CMO. These questions covered both quantitative and qualitative aspects to guide the subsequent analysis and presentation.

Task 2 – Data Cleaning, Analysis & Visualization:
Processed and cleaned the dataset to ensure accuracy. Created four Power BI dashboards addressing the specific questions from the leadership team:

Monthly Revenue Trends (2011): Time-series visualization to identify seasonal patterns and assist the CEO with revenue forecasting.

Top 10 Countries by Revenue (Excluding UK): Comparative analysis for the CMO, showing both revenue and quantity sold to identify high-performing markets.

Top 10 Customers by Revenue: Ranking visualization to help the CMO focus on top clients and enhance customer retention strategies.

Global Demand Analysis (Excluding UK): Comprehensive map visualization highlighting high-demand regions to guide the CEO’s expansion plans.

Task 3 – Executive Presentation:
Developed a professional presentation summarizing insights and recommendations. Recorded a video walkthrough explaining the findings, dashboards, and business implications for both the CEO and CMO.

Tools Used:

Microsoft Excel – Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Power BI – Data Visualization & Dashboarding

Microsoft PowerPoint – Insight Presentation

Skills Gained:
Data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business intelligence, storytelling with data, executive communication, and strategic insight generation.
